About Us

Our Story

Mountain House Science Olympiad focuses on empowering its students to excel in the field of science and technology through hands-on learning experiences and collaborative projects. We use Science Olympiad as a method to foster a love for STEM subjects and encourage critical thinking skills, while having fun by traveling across the state for competitions. It is an amazing experience, so feel free to join!
